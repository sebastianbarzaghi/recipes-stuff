Pad Thai

Description

Pad thai, phat thai, or phad thai, is a stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food in Thailand as part of the country's cuisine.

It is typically made with rice noodles, shrimp, peanuts, a scrambled egg, and bean sprouts, among other vegetables. The ingredients are fried in a wok.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Shrimp
  • Peanuts
  • Egg
  • Bean sprouts
